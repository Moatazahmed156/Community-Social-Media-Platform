# 🐝 Hive

> **Where Communities Connect.**

Hive is a community-driven social platform that enables organizations, clubs, universities, teams, and communities to communicate, collaborate, and share information in a secure and organized environment.

Unlike traditional social media platforms, Hive focuses on **group-based communication**, allowing users to create or join communities where content is moderated before publication. This ensures that announcements, discussions, and updates remain relevant, trustworthy, and valuable to all members.

---

# 🌟 Overview

Hive provides a centralized platform where communities can easily manage communication, share updates, organize discussions, and encourage collaboration among members.

Each community is managed through a **role-based permission system**, allowing Owners and Admins to moderate content while Members actively participate by creating posts, commenting, and reacting.

Whether it's a university club, company department, volunteer team, or student organization, Hive provides the tools needed to keep everyone connected.

## 🛡️ Role-Based Access Control

Each community supports multiple roles:

- **Owner**
  - Creates and manages the community.
  - Assigns or removes administrators.
  - Has full control over community settings.

- **Admin**
  - Reviews and approves posts.
  - Manages members.
  - Helps moderate community activity.

- **Member**
  - Creates posts.
  - Comments on discussions.
  - Reacts to posts.
  - Participates in community activities.
